Privacy Policy

Effective date: 1 January 2026  |  Last updated: 27 February 2026

Blinkdrop Technologies Pvt. Ltd. ("Blinkdrop," "we," "our," "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our smart home devices, web platform, and services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Account Data: Your name, email address, and hashed password when you register on the Blinkdrop portal.
  • Device Data: Names, states (on/off, speed), and configuration of your paired smart home devices.
  • Usage Data: Energy consumption readings, device action history (e.g., who turned on a device at what time), and automation schedule configurations.
  • Technical Data: Your IP address, browser type, operating system, and usage timestamps collected automatically by our backend.
  • Session Tokens: JWT tokens containing your user ID and token version, stored locally in your browser for authentication.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide and improve our services:

  • Authentication: Your credentials are verified against our backend API at https://smart-home-04m4.onrender.com using industry-standard JWT-based authentication.
  • Device Control: Commands are relayed to your devices via our MQTT broker in real time.
  • Energy Analytics: Usage history is stored to generate your energy consumption reports and cost projections.
  • Google Home Integration: When you link Blinkdrop to Google Home, your device states are shared with Google's Smart Home API to enable voice control. We only share the minimum data necessary.
  • Security: We use token versioning to detect and invalidate compromised sessions across all devices.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on MongoDB Atlas servers. We apply the following security measures:

  • Passwords are hashed using bcrypt before storage — we never store plaintext passwords.
  • All API communications use HTTPS with TLS encryption.
  • MQTT communications use authentication tokens and are secured using TLS.
  • JWT tokens are signed with a server-side secret key and include a token version to prevent replay attacks after logout.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Google LLC: When you use Google Home integration, device states and names are shared with Google as required for the Smart Home Actions integration.
  • Service Providers: Our infrastructure providers (MongoDB Atlas, Render.com, Vercel) may process your data as sub-processors under their privacy policies.
  • Legal Compliance: We may disclose data if required by Indian law, court order, or governmental authority.

5. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Device action history is retained for up to 12 months. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time by emailing support@blinkdrop.in.

6. Your Rights

Under the Information Technology Act, 2000 and the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (India), you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by us.
  • Correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Request deletion of your personal data.
  • Withdraw consent for data processing (note: this may limit service functionality).

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@blinkdrop.in.

7. Cookies

Our website uses essential cookies only for maintaining your login session and theme preference. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for use by individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of material changes via email or an in-app notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance.

10. Contact Us

For privacy-related queries, contact us at info@blinkdrop.in. For account or device support, email support@blinkdrop.in. Or write to us at Blinkdrop Technologies Pvt. Ltd., Bengaluru, Karnataka, India — 560001.
